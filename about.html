Nice to meet you

Hi there, I'm Fleur. I'm a freelance artist based in Sussex. My heart lies in Newquay - I moved to Cornwall after studying fashion and fell in love with its unique scenery and culture. I started De Mort to bring Newquay’s buzzing skate and surf scene to streetwear. 

And the little guy? That's Trigger, my persistent assistant.

Ashes to ashes

La Fleur De Mort means the 'The Flower of Death'. I’m an artist who works on deep conceptual topics and I’m passionate about the messages that my work portrays. 

My style is heavily influenced by pop-art. I love capturing moments and vibes of ​​​everyday home and street life in strong, punchy ways. 

Want to know more? 

I'd love to hear from you! You can drop me a line on the contact page or on social media. You can also see the stuff I get up to on Instagram. 

I sell a small range of merchandise through Etsy, with favourites shown on the Products page. 

If you'd like to commission a piece, please get in touch.
